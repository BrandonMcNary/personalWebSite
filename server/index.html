About Me


My name is Brandon and I am a Software Engineer. After many years working as a financier in Private Equity, I decided that the greener pastures of Software Engineering were for me. Without a job, or placement in Hack Reactor, I took my family across the country with me to pursue this dream. It was worth the risk. Looking back, it’s a hell of a lot more exciting now that the possibility of changing the world is literally at my fingertips. Plus, throwing your hoodie into the wash is a fraction of what dry-cleaning that suit every week is! In truth, I didn’t move to this field for the excitement, or my dry cleaning bill (definite pluses though). I made the transition because there are an infinite number of interesting things one can learn in this space. Further, I love that in order to stay fresh and relevant in this industry that moves at light speed, you need to continue progressing forward. Whether it’s learning a new framework, or a new language altogether, I look forward to the growth that is sure to come in my new home

Thanks for stopping by!
